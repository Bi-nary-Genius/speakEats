

# SpeakEats

It started in my kitchen. I was standing at the counter, staring into my pantry, typing ingredients into ChatGPT one by one trying to figure out what to cook for dinner. It was tedious, slow, and honestly annoying. I thought — there has to be a better way to do this. So I built one.

SpeakEats is a voice and text powered meal discovery app built for the everyday person who wants to cook something new without overthinking it. Whether you are working with a full fridge or stretching your last twenty dollars, SpeakEats meets you where you are.

## The Story Behind It

Most meal apps assume you have everything. SpeakEats assumes you have whatever is left in your kitchen and helps you make something delicious out of it. It is built for the person who is budget conscious, creatively hungry, and tired of the same five meals on rotation.

## What You Can Do

Speak It — Tap the mic or type your ingredients and SpeakEats suggests four meal ideas based on exactly what you have. Switch into Moody Foodie mode if you want personalized suggestions based on your cuisine mood, how much time you have, and how hungry you are.

Good Eat'n — Save the meals you love, come back after cooking, rate them with reactions like Damn this was Good or Obsessed, and add personal notes. Your saved recipes live here like a personal food journal.

Budget Planner — Tell SpeakEats your grocery budget, your must haves, and your zip code. It builds you a smart grocery list, estimates your total spend, and recommends the most affordable stores near you with Google Maps links.

## Tech Stack

React, Vite, Express, Claude API, Web Speech API

## How To Run It

Clone the repo, run npm install, create a .env file and add your Anthropic API key as ANTHROPIC_API_KEY, then run npm run dev. The app runs on localhost 5173.
